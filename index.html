About Me

I am a Full-Stack developer with deep expertise in web development and design. I create modern, user-friendly, and efficient websites by combining programming and UI/UX design. I am proficient in JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and SCSS, working with Git to build adaptive and high-performance web applications. I use Figma for interface design, focusing on both usability and aesthetics. Beyond web development, I also work with graphics and animation. I use Blender for 3D modeling, After Effects for animations, Illustrator and Photoshop for design creation, and Premiere Pro for video editing. My ability to blend coding with visual artistry allows me to develop unique digital products that are not only functional but also visually appealing. I am always open to new challenges and strive to create innovative and high-quality solutions.
